Pizza

Description

This pizza is protein packed and will keep you full for hours after consuming it, and only requires a few ingredients

Ingredients

  • greek yogurt
  • self rising flour
  • italian herbs
  • fat free cheese
  • skim cheese

Steps

  1. mix greek yogurt, self rising flour, and italian herb seasoning
  2. mix until dough ball is created
  3. add cheese
  4. bake for 22 minutes at 400F
